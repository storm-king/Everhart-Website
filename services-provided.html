Overview of Services

At Everhart Steeple Services, we offer the services necessary to keep your church looking brand new. If you have any questions, or would like a free quote, please visit the Contact Us page.

Please note, in order to properly quote your request you must enter information about the size and condition of the church. This gives us an idea of the proper equipment needed.

Power Washing

Everhart Steeple Services offers complete exterior church steeple cleaning services from fiberglass steeples to historical brick, stone, and other hard to reach areas.

Our foundation for this service is based both on proper cleaning methods and environmentally friendly materials. We safely clean services to remove mold, lichen, and other airborne contaminants.

Painting

Everhart Steeple Services takes pride in our high-quality painting services. Our experienced crew knows how to safely access your structure in order to provide thorough surface cleaning, and any associated repairs. After the preparation is complete, our crew is trained to properly apply the highest quality protective coating. We pay close attention to detail to ensure that your structure is admired for a lifetime.
